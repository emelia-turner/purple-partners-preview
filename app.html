The Purple app

One app. Every kind of connection.

Staff, guests, and residents all connect through the same Purple app. It's the engine behind identity-based access, the layer that removes captive-portal friction, and the channel that keeps every connection going after the first login.

One app, three jobsThe same app authenticates staff, recognizes returning guests, and provisions resident networks, depending on which Purple product is deployed.
Nothing for IT to installThe app lives on the end user's own device. There's no agent to roll out and no extra hardware to manage.
Built on identity, not passwordsEvery connection through the app is tied to a verified identity, not a shared credential that has to be rotated or reset.
