Multi-tenant WiFi

Every resident. Their own private network.

From BTR developments to student accommodation and aparthotels, Purple's multi-tenant WiFi gives every occupant a secure, isolated connection that feels like home broadband, provisioned through the Purple app.

What your customers get

The device isolation problem, solvedPurple creates a personal Private Area Network (PAN) for each user. They log in once, and all their devices are placed inside a secure, private bubble that follows them around the venue.
True privacy in shared environmentsEvery user sits in their own micro-segment, so they can only ever see their own devices, essential for a student in Room 101 never seeing the devices of the student in Room 102.
Seamless setup for all devicesUsers authenticate once via the Purple app. Browserless devices like consoles and smart TVs connect with a unique iPSK passcode, and everything reconnects automatically on return.
The app as an engagement channelSurveys, location-aware content, notifications, and rewards delivered directly to residents and guests through the app.

FAQ

Frequently asked questions.

In multi-tenant environments like student housing, hotels, or care homes, Purple creates a personal Private Area Network for each user. They log in once, and all their devices sit inside a secure, private bubble that follows them around the venue, so residents get a home-like experience with enterprise security.

Tenant isolation uses technologies like VLANs, Dynamic PSK (iPSK), and 802.1X to ensure that even though tenants share the same physical access points, their traffic is completely separated. Devices on one tenant's network cannot see or communicate with devices on another's.
