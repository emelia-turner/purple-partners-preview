Multi-tenant WiFi

Every resident. Their own private network.

From Build-to-Rent (BTR) developments to student accommodation and aparthotels, Purple's multi-tenant WiFi gives every occupant a secure, isolated connection that feels like home broadband, provisioned through the Purple app.

What your customers get

The device isolation problem, solved

Purple creates a personal Private Area Network (PAN) for each user. They log in once, and all their devices are placed inside a secure, private bubble that follows them around the venue.

True privacy in shared environments

Tenant isolation uses VLANs, Dynamic PSK (xPSK), and 802.1X, so even though tenants share the same physical access points, their traffic is completely separated. A student in Room 101 can never see the devices of the student in Room 102.

Seamless setup for all devices

Users authenticate once via the Purple app. Browserless devices like consoles and smart TVs connect with a unique xPSK passcode, and everything reconnects automatically on return.

The app as an engagement channel

The required network authentication step doubles as a two-way communication platform, surveys, location-aware content, notifications, and rewards, delivered directly to residents and guests.

Operator dashboard

Manage every unit, building, and development from a single cloud dashboard, with insights into building utilization, see which wings or blocks are most active.
