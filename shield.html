Shield

Network-level protection your customers can actually show.

DNS-layer content filtering and real-time threat blocking, built into the WiFi platform. Add a compelling compliance and safeguarding story to education, healthcare, and public sector deals.

Why it works.

DNS-layer filtering

Block harmful content categories at the network level, before it reaches any device.

Threats identified up to 10 days sooner

Shield's AI-driven filtering identifies threats up to 10 days before they hit standard databases, blocking zero-day domains other filters miss.

Customisable category controls

Customers choose what's blocked, by network, by VLAN, by time of day.

Exportable reports showing filtering activity. IWF and Friendly WiFi certified, with evidence built for council and regulatory audits.
