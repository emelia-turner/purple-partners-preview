Resorts World Birmingham

Drove guest engagement and data capture while reducing manual marketing effort.

The story

Challenge, solution, result.

Challenge

Resorts World Birmingham had no way to tailor WiFi by venue or audience across its casinos, hotel, spa, and leisure venues, content management was manual, and the business couldn't capture usable marketing data.

Solution

Purple deployed across 328 Cisco Meraki access points, using LogicFlow for automated audience segmentation and custom splash pages for seasonal campaigns, with data syncing directly into the CRM.

Result

Since the July 2022 launch, guest connectivity complaints have been virtually eliminated, and the marketing team saves days of manual effort per campaign.
