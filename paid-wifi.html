Paid WiFi

Boost your customers' revenue and guest experience with Paid WiFi.

Paid WiFi is included in the Connect, Capture, or Engage plan, creating a premium visitor experience for your customer's guests while boosting their revenue and saving on bandwidth.

Open a new revenue stream for your customers with Paid WiFi.

Tiered bandwidth packages

Offer packages by speed, time, or data allowance, so guests can pick the option that suits them.

Purchase longer or faster WiFi

Guests can upgrade to a longer session or a faster connection whenever they need it, on top of the free tier already in place.

Included on every plan

Available on Connect, Capture, and Engage, including the free Connect plan, so there's no separate contract to set up.

A cost line becomes a revenue line

Guests who want a premium experience pay for it directly, instead of the venue absorbing the full bandwidth cost alone.

How it works

Power up with Paid WiFi in four steps.

1
Get your customer onto Connect, Capture, or Engage
Activate Paid WiFi on any plan, including the free Connect offering.
2
Set up your customer's hardware and WiFi login
Guests are directed to a variety of payment options for Paid WiFi.
3
Set up your customer's Paid WiFi plan
Your customer chooses the plan for premium users, by speed, time, or data allowance.
4
Your customer starts earning from day one
Once the Paid WiFi plan is published, revenue starts as soon as guests purchase.
