Beyond the portal

Or skip the portal entirely: identity-based connectivity via the Purple app.

Visitors opt in once through the Purple app, and every connection after that is identity-verified and automatic, no login screen, no re-entering details.

Identity-verified from the first packet

No open SSIDs, no anonymous access. WPA2/3-Enterprise secures every connection before network access is granted.

No portal friction for returners

Once opted in, the app recognizes the visitor automatically on every future visit, to any venue in the chain.

Still a verified contact for Capture and Engage

Every identity-based connection is a verified, opted-in contact, feeding the same CRM sync and marketing automation.

80% fewer IT support tickets

Eliminating captive-portal login issues removes one of the most common sources of on-site WiFi support requests.
