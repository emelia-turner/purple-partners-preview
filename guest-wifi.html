Captive portal

A branded captive portal, built in.

The portal overlays directly onto a customer's existing hardware, with no infrastructure changes and nothing to rip out.

Fully branded splash pages
Logo, colors, and imagery match the customer's brand, not Purple's.
25+ supported languages
One portal, ready for visitors in their own language out of the box.
Live in hours
Deploys as a cloud overlay on supported hardware. No new access points, no rip-and-replace.
Security built in
Verify and Shield sit underneath the portal, so the login isn't just branded, it's clean and protected too.
Beyond the portal

Or skip the portal entirely: identity-based connectivity via the Purple app.

Visitors opt in once through the Purple app, and every connection after that is identity-verified and automatic, no login screen, no re-entering details.

Easy data sharing
Every login syncs automatically with the systems a client already runs, no manual exports, no extra integration work.
A clean, usable database
Verify checks every email in real time at the point of login, so fake and disposable addresses never reach the CRM.
Powerful analytics
Every connection feeds real-time usage, footfall, and engagement data a client can act on immediately.
CRM integration, built in
Every identity-based connection is a verified, opted-in contact, syncing straight into the CRM a client already runs.
Personalization at scale
Location-aware content, tailored offers, and targeted messaging, delivered to the right person at the right moment.
