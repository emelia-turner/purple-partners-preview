FAQ

Frequently asked questions.

Staff WiFi is an employee-only wireless network authenticated per-user, isolated from the guest and payment networks. Where guest WiFi optimizes for low-friction sign-up, staff WiFi optimizes for identity - each employee authenticates with their own credential via 802.1X against a RADIUS server, and their access can be revoked the moment they leave the company without touching anyone else on the network.

No. Purple operates the RADIUS server as a cloud service - RADIUS-as-a-Service - with multi-region failover and a 99.999% uptime SLA. Your access points point at Purple, Purple validates credentials against your identity provider, and no one on the customer's team operates RADIUS infrastructure.

Yes, instantly. Because staff WiFi is per-user, disabling the employee in the identity provider revokes their WiFi access at the next authentication attempt. Compare this to a shared WiFi password, where one departing employee forces a company-wide password rotation.

Yes. Purple runs on any enterprise-grade access point that speaks RADIUS - Cisco Meraki, Cisco Catalyst, Aruba, Ruckus, Juniper Mist, Ubiquiti UniFi, Cambium, Extreme, Fortinet FortiAP, and more. No hardware replacement, just SSID reconfiguration to authenticate via Purple.

Yes - multi-site estates are where Purple fits best. Because the cloud RADIUS service and identity-provider integration are centralized, customers roll out the same passwordless 802.1X policy across every site from a single dashboard, with no per-site RADIUS hardware to ship or maintain.
